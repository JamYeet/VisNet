About Our Team

We are a team of three passionate individuals committed to improving accessibility and interaction through visual speech recognition.

VisNet was developed as part of our research into AI and deep learning systems that can understand human communication without relying on audio. Our goal is to create technology that breaks down barriers and makes communication more accessible for everyone.

Our team combines expertise in computer vision, deep learning, and accessibility technologies. We've worked tirelessly to create an algorithm that can recognize speech from visual cues with increasing accuracy, and we're committed to continuous improvement of our system.

Our Mission

To develop cutting-edge visual speech recognition technology that enhances communication accessibility, improves security capabilities, and provides innovative solutions for various industries.
